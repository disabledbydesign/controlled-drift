Controlled Drift · one app
One app, one set of tabs
The daily overlay and the review/reorganize editor are now one program over one Anytype database. Today is the generated plan you act on; Add captures and sorts; Map is the goal→project→task tree; Routines and Strategies are their own tabs; Focus holds this week and upcoming periods, authored with a single confirm. Tap a plan item’s project — or a capture’s “verify” — to drop into Map and land right back where you were.

The window tracks what you're doing. Today, Focus and Add stay a phone-width column on the right; Map expands leftward one panel at a time as you drill Goals → Projects → Tasks, with the editor docked on the right. Selecting an item opens its full fields there — same rose selection as the phone, no resize jump.
